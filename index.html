About the Player

Forged on the
World Stage

Brijesh Yadav is an Indian international badminton player and professional coach who has represented India on major global stages. Specialising in Men's Doubles and Mixed Doubles, Brijesh climbed to a career-high BWF World Ranking of #68 and an Asian Ranking of #45.

A product of the elite Pullela Gopichand Badminton Academy in Hyderabad, Brijesh has worn the Indian jersey at the Thomas Cup, Sudirman Cup, Badminton Asia Championships, and BWF circuit events across Thailand, Egypt, Norway, Zambia, and South Africa.
